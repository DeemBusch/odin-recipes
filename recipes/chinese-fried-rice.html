Chinese-American Fried Rice

Description

Chinese-American fried rice is a versatile, nutrient rich, and balanced one-pot meal option to serve as an accessible side or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 Bags (~5 cups) Microwave Jasmine Rice
  • 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1/4-1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • Diced Onions
  • Diced Garlic
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Seed Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pot on stove and sautee onions, garlic, eggs, and vegetables.
