Vegetable Fried Rice

Description

Chinese-American style fried rice is a versatile, nutrient rich, and balanced one-pot meal option to serve as a side or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 Bags (~5 cups) Microwave Jasmine Rice
  • 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1/4-1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • Diced Onions
  • Diced Garlic
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Seed Oil
  • 2-4 eggs

Steps

  1. Heat oil in medium sized pot on medium-low heat.
  2. Saute onions, garlic, eggs, and vegetables.
  3. Add rice, soy sauce, sesame seed oil and cook over medium-low heat until she bussin.
  4. Serve warm or let cool (this is where you can get creative), just dig the fork in!
