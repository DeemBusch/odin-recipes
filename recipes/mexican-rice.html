Mexican Rice

Description

Mexican style rice is a versatile, nutrient rich, and balanced one-pot meal option to serve as a side or main dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 Bags (~5 cups) Microwave Basmati Rice
  • 2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1/4-1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • Diced Onions
  • Diced Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Water
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat oil in medium sized pot on medium-low heat.
  2. Toast rice and saute onions, garlic, and vegetables.
  3. Add rice and tomato sauce and cook covered over low heat until she bussin.
  4. Serve warm or let cool (this is where you can get creative), just dig the fork in!
